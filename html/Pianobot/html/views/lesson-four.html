Lesson 4

Objective

In this example the Linkbot will play changing tones a fixed number of times while the duration remains constant. The Linkbot can also change tone and duration a fixed number of times. Make the Linkbot change tone and duration to make interesting sounds.

#!/usr/bin/env python3

# Filename: Lesson04.py
import linkbot
import time

robot = linkbot.Linkbot('')

t=1
for i in range (33,53,2):  #change the value i by steps of 2
    k=pow(2,(i-49)/12)*440  #sets tone depending on the value i
    robot.setBuzzerFrequency(k)
    t=t-.1 # shortens the duration of the note
    time.sleep(t)
    robot.setBuzzerFrequency(0)
    time.sleep(t)
