Lesson 3

Objective

Alarms or signals sometimes beep a fixed number of times. Explore how to make the Linkbot beep a fixed number of times and then go silent. Make the Linkbot beep five times with a duration of 1 second.

#!/usr/bin/env python3

# Filename: Lesson03.py
import linkbot
import time

robot = linkbot.Linkbot('')

for i in range (5):
    robot.setBuzzerFrequency(4186.01)
    time.sleep(1)
    robot.setBuzzerFrequency(0)
    time.sleep(1)
